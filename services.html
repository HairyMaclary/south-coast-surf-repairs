Jeremy Buis Surfcraft Dunedin New Zealand

Epoxy, snaps, dings, fins, plugs, fibreglass, paddles, boats ... just whatever.

Buis surfcraft is another fine example of a back-yard Kiwi business getting the job done. After over 20 years of repairing and shaping surf boards you see it all!

Using quality materials I offer a fast and friendly service with flexible drop off and pick up times. I repair a wide range of water craft; surfboards and club boards to everything in between. I can replace FCS plugs, glass on fins, snaps, creases, large and small dings. Basically any board damage. No worries on polyester and epoxy boards too. Half of the boards I repair are now epoxy.

Heaps of Fibreglass options are also available, with experience in windsurfers, SUP paddles, boats, car parts, and more.

Your ideas count! For example if your board snaps do you want the stringer replaced or just an extra layer of glass? Let's talk so you can get what you want!

Many customers like sun-bake UV curing resin, which considerably speeds up the curing process while creating a stronger repair. This means your board will be finished much faster. With UV curing resin urgent repairs can be finished in 24 hours. My aim is to get you and your board back in the water quickly for an affordable price. I will beat any genuine quote.
